{% regionblock accommodations_header_3, type:header %}
The charming Wine Country Inn features beautiful vineyard views, comfortable rooms and quaint cottages unlike any other boutique hotel in the Napa Valley, as well as the highest-quality bedding and in-room amenities for a relaxing getaway in California wine country.
{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock accommodations_text_1, type:text %}

Located in the heart of wine country, Wine Country Inn & Cottages is within minutes of world-class wineries, Michelin-starred restaurants, and one-of-a-kind boutique shopping. Yet, this tucked away boutique hotel also allows guests the privilege of a relaxing getaway with its comfortable rooms and soothing amenities.

At the Wine Country Inn travelers can choose from 5 quaint cottages, 4 spacious suites, and 20 cozy guestrooms. Accommodations at the inn offer the finest quality bedding and amenities, while most rooms feature either a private patio or deck to indulge in vineyard views with a glass of Napa Valley wine. Select rooms also feature spas.

Each reservation at Wine Country Inn provides free parking and complimentary WiFi, as well as a complimentary country breakfast served daily, complete with local fruits, fresh-baked artisanal breads, and specialty quiches. In addition, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages offers weekly wine tastings, making for an authentic Napa Valley experience.
