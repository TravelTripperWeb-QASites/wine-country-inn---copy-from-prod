Dining

Mouth-watering experiences we can highly recommend

When you’re on vacation, food isn’t just a necessity; it’s part of the experience. Here in Napa Valley, you can dive into stick-to-your-ribs fare at a roadside stand or cafe, or indulge in phenomenal cuisine at world-renowned restaurants. During your stay at the Wine Country Inn & Cottages, the staff can recommend an array of mouth-watering restaurants, no matter what you’re craving.

Before you make your reservation at this Napa Valley boutique hotel, download a complimentary Vacation Guide and start perusing the Wine Country Inn & Cottages’ top recommendations. For even more info, visit our blog.


For travelers who want to customize the experience from start to finish, be sure to inquire about our Wine Country Experience package.


Restaurants located in St. Helena are just 5 minutes south of the Wine Country Inn & Cottages. The town of Calistoga is 7 miles north, while Yountville is located 9 miles south. Please note that most restaurants open their books only one to two months in advance, and advance reservations are recommended.

Archetype ~ (707) 968-9200 Locally farmed American meals are the draw at this stylish neighborhood eatery with outdoor seating.

Auberge du Soleil ~ (707) 963-1211 Upscale fine dining restaurant with an exceptional view of the Napa Valley from both the bar and dining room. Great for lunch also. Located 5 miles South on Silverado Trail.

Brasswood Bar + Kitchen ~ (707-302-5101) – A gathering place for locals and visitors alike serves farm to table wine country comfort food.
