The Wine Country Inn & Cottages' convenient location between St. Helena and Calistoga is within walking distance of famous wineries and a short drive from the Napa Valley’s most important attractions, including the Culinary Institute of America. Twenty-nine comfortable guestrooms and suites bring together traditional design and country charm with panoramic views of the Napa Valley wine district. With so many comfortable rooms and suites at this boutique bed and breakfast, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages is the ideal St. Helena location for many group sizes, small or large.
