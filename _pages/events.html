Groups & Events

A relaxing Napa Valley retreat for every occasion

Enjoy any special business or social event in California’s scenic Napa Valley wine country at the Wine Country Inn & Cottages. The Inn’s pleasant and professional staff will ensure an unforgettable meeting or special occasion in an intimate wine country setting. Indoor and outdoor spaces are perfect for elopements, workshops, family reunions, and special celebrations.

The Wine Country Inn & Cottages' convenient location between St. Helena and Calistoga is within walking distance of famous wineries and a short drive from the Napa Valley’s most important attractions, including the Culinary Institute of America. Twenty-nine comfortable guestrooms and suites bring together traditional design and country charm with panoramic views of the Napa Valley wine district. With so many comfortable rooms and suites at this boutique bed and breakfast, the Wine Country Inn & Cottages is the ideal St. Helena location for many group sizes, small or large. The Living Room is a wonderful gathering place, as well, with comfortable seating and attractive furnishings. Along with the Living Room, the exterior grounds feature large patios, broad walkways, and outdoor spaces with beautiful vineyard views and mountain vistas, which provide a great backdrop for weddings and family reunions.

If suddenly eloping to the beautiful Napa Valley or leisurely planning a large group gathering or business retreat, the staff at Wine Country Inn & Cottages will help make any event memorable. At the Wine Country Inn & Cottages, it is understood how important it is to make every event special. As a result, there are a number of meeting packages and elopement packages to help enhance any event and make every occasion even more special.
