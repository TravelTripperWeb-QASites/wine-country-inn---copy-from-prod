Welcome to wine country

Guests of the luxurious Wine Country Inn & Cottages enjoy some of the finest accommodations in Napa Valley. This romantic, secluded St. Helena bed and breakfast provides easy access to world-class vineyards and restaurants across Napa Valley. Not to mention, guests are within steps, or a short drive, from a nearly endless list of things to do and see.

Surrounded by rolling hills and postcard-worthy views, our St. Helena hotel is perfect for a Napa Valley honeymoon, rejuvenating escape, or a romantic getaway. It’s also ideal for a family retreat or reunion.

Escape to luxury in the heart of the Napa Valley

The accommodations at the Wine Country Inn & Cottages allow guests to rest, relax, and rejuvenate in the intimate privacy of a longtime Napa Valley hideaway, where unique architecture meshes with surrounding vineyards for that perfect wine country experience.

All Accommodations

The Wine Country Inn & Cottages is recognized as one of the best St. Helena bed and breakfasts in Napa Valley. With 29 comfortable guestrooms and suites, the inn merges traditional design and country charm, making it one of the most unique boutique hotels near Napa Valley. Don’t miss the chance to take a dip in the pool or spend a luxurious afternoon in the seasonal outdoor spa. Take some time to explore the property of our St. Helena hotel, which includes lush landscape, open lawns, and shaded nooks. Your reward is a picture perfect panoramic view of the Napa Valley wine district.
